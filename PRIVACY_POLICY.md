## 4. Cookies

This Application uses the following cookies:

| Cookie Name          | Type        | Duration    | Purpose                              |
|----------------------|-------------|-------------|--------------------------------------|
| `gdpr_consent`       | Functional  | 365 days    | Stores your cookie consent decision  |
| `session_id`         | Strictly necessary | Session | Maintains app session              |
| `analytics_opt_in`  | Analytics   | 365 days    | Records if you opted into analytics  |

**Strictly necessary cookies** are set automatically. All other cookies require your **explicit consent** via the cookie popup.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or using the cookie settings panel within the Application.

- **Art. 20** — Right to data portability
